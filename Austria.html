Austria

Continent-Europe. Capital-Vienna. Population Cap-8.8 million (As of 2019)

Legal Information

Cannabis (Weed) and other drugs are legal in Austria for medical use, but not for recreational use.


You must be 16 or older to drink beer, 18 or older to drink distilled beverages


A driver's license is mandatory to drive.


Drivers stay on the right side of the road.


Currency-Euro


Mainly speak German


Use the metric system


Some webpages may be blocked in Austria- click here for more information.


Austria permits adults over 18 years of age to buy guns for hunting and self-defense.
