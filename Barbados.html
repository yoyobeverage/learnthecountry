Barbados

Legal Information

Marijuana, cocaine, and estacy are illegal in Barbados.


You must be 16 to drink beer, and 18 to work at a bar


You must be 21 years or older to drive, Rental companies usually require 3-5 years of driving


Drivers stay on the right side of the road


Currency-BBD(Barbadian dollar)


Official language is English


Use the Metric system
