Legal Information

Drugs are illegal in Turkey, except for Marijuana, which is legal in some bars


You must be 18 or older to drink beer, 18 or older to drink distilled beverages

A driver's license is mandatory to drive.


Drivers stay on the right side of the road.


Currency-Turkish Lira


Mainly speak Turkish, Armenian


Use the metric system


Turkey permits adults over 25 to buy guns, hunting is generally illegal except for a small portion of land.
